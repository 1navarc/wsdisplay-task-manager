Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

WSDisplay Email ("the App") is a team email collaboration platform operated by WS Display. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Email Data: We access your Gmail messages (read and send) to display and manage them within the App. This data is synced and stored in our secure database.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the App, such as conversations viewed, assignments made, and tags applied.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the email collaboration service (syncing, displaying, and sending emails)
  • To enable team features like assignment, tagging, and internal notes
  • To authenticate your identity and manage your session
  • To improve the App's functionality and user experience

Google API Services

WSDisplay Email's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored on Google Cloud Platform (Cloud SQL and Cloud Run) with encryption at rest and in transit.
  • Access to the database is restricted to authorized services only.
  • Session data is stored securely with automatic expiration.
  • We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data Retention

Email data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Session data expires automatically after 7 days.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data stored in the App
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke the App's access to your Google account at any time via Google Account Permissions
